Slieve Commedagh

Slieve Commedagh stands at 767 metres(2,516 ft) and is the 2nd tallest peak. It is about a 4 hours hike and you can start from Donard car park. From there folow the path up to the green gate, then follow that path until you reach the saddle (the area that Slieve Commedagh and Slieve Donard connect at). Turn right when on the saddle and now you are climbing Slieve Commedagh.
