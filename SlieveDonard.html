Slieve Donard

Slieve Donard stands at 850 metres (2,790 ft) and is the tallest peak of the Mourne Mountains. Start at Donard car park. From their follow the path into the forest and you should be alongside the Glen river. You'll be crossing two bridges from there then the ascent towards the saddle will be very noticeable. Once on the saddle, take a left and now you will be climbing towards the peak of Donard. Be precautious when climbing from the saddle to the peak, it can be very dangerous and slipp, so wear appropiate footwear for safety measures and stick close to the wall for extra leverage. The view up there is the best I have every witnessed my whole life, it is truely view to remember when overlooking Newcastle and Murlough beach. Below is a nice video of a small waterfall.
