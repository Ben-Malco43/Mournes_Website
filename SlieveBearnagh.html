Slieve Bearnagh

Slieve Bearnagh stands at 739 metres(2,425 ft) and is the 4th tallest peak. It is about a 4/5hrs hike and you can start from Trassey car park or the Meelmore lodge. From there follow the forest path and along the Trassey river to top of Hare's Gap (this saddle between Slieve Bearnagh & Slievenaglogh). Turn right when on the saddle and now you are climbing Slieve Bearnagh.
