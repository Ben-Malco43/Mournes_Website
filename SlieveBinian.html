Slieve Binian

Slieve Binian stands at 746 metres(2,448 ft) and is the 3rd tallest peak. It is about a 2.5 hour hike and you can start from Carrick Little Car Park. From there follow the stone track, cross the stone wall, and follow the mourne wall. When the wall starts to rise you are now at the bottom of slieve binian. Note that this is a very difficult climb so make sure to be safe and use your hands to move from rock to rock.
