Slieve Meelmore

Slieve Meelmore stands at 680 metres (2,230 ft) and is the 7th tallest peak. Start at Ott car park, and cross the stile on the opposite side of the road. After about 140 metres the path splits, tsake the upper path and follow that path until you meet the mourne wall. Turn left and follow the wall towards the summit of Slive Meelmore.
