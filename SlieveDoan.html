Slieve Doan

Slieve Doan stands at 593 metres (1,946 ft) and is the 16th tallest peak. It is about a 2 hours hike and you can start from Ott car park on the slievenaman road. From there cross the road, and climb over the stile. Once you reach a stone path continue towards the saddle between the two mountains. Now keep LoughShannagh on your right and Slieve Doan should now be in your sights. This mountain is not part of the six peak challenge although it is one of my favourite mountains. The view is spectacular and the view of LoughShannagh is beautiful, this mountain I recommend everyone does atleast once in their lifetime.
