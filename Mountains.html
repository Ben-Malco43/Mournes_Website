Did You Know
The Mourne Mountains are the tallest mountain ranges in the whole of Northern Ireland and they were created over 50 million years ago. Although they were not created by man, they are made of granite and igneous rock. They were formed due to alot of volcanic activity after the time of the dinosaurs. The mountains are now made out of molten magma froms the earth's inner core. See the layout of the Earth's core below. The earth is made of four layers: inner core, outer core, mantle and crust.

Did You Know
The Mourne Mountains was used by smugglers in the 1700s and 1800s. Criminals smuggled wine, tobacco, leather and spirits through cargo ships. If you can imagine a camel carrying luggage around the desert, this was very like what these smugglers would do. They would use horses to transport there goods and this is why there are paths carved out throughout the Mourne Mountains which we still use today but not for smuggling in products, but to climb these mountains.
